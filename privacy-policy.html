1. Introduction

Welcome to ShedSync ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our portable building inventory management platform.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Account Information: Business name, full name, email address, phone number, business address
  • Inventory Data: Building information, photos, pricing, serial numbers
  • Business Settings: Operating hours, social media links, website customization preferences
  • Payment Information: Processed securely through Stripe (we do not store credit card details)

2.2 Information from Third-Party Services

When you connect third-party services to ShedSync:

  • Facebook: When you connect your Facebook page, we receive your page ID, page name, and access token to post on your behalf
  • Inventory Management Systems: Credentials you provide to sync your inventory (stored encrypted)

2.3 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: How you interact with our platform, features used, time spent
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system
  • Cookies: We use cookies for authentication and to improve your experience

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the ShedSync platform
  • Sync your inventory from connected systems
  • Automatically post building updates to your Facebook page (when enabled)
  • Process payments and manage subscriptions
  • Send service notifications and updates
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve and develop new features
  • Ensure platform security and prevent fraud

4. How We Share Your Information

4.1 Public Information

The following information is publicly visible on your dealer website:

  • Business name, phone number, address (if provided)
  • Building inventory (photos, descriptions, pricing)
  • Operating hours and social media links
  • Any customization you apply to your public site

4.2 Service Providers

We share information with trusted third-party service providers:

  • Stripe: For payment processing
  • Vercel: For hosting and infrastructure
  • Database Providers: For secure data storage

4.3 Facebook

When you enable Facebook auto-posting, we post building information to your Facebook page on your behalf using the permissions you grant.

4.4 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or to protect our rights and safety.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/SSL)
  • Password hashing using bcrypt
  • Encrypted storage of sensitive credentials
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and authentication

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all personal information we store about you
  • Update: Correct or update your information in your account settings
  • Delete: Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Export: Download your inventory data
  • Opt-out: Disable Facebook auto-posting or other automated features
  • Cancel: Cancel your subscription at any time

7. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. If you cancel your account:

  • Your data will be deleted within 30 days
  • Your public website will be taken down immediately
  • Billing records may be retained for tax/legal purposes (up to 7 years)

8. Children's Privacy

ShedSync is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18.

9. Third-Party Links

Our platform may contain links to third-party websites (e.g., Facebook, inventory management systems). We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Please review their privacy policies.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by:

  • Posting the new policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending you an email notification (for material changes)

11. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights:

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to know if personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Right to say no to the sale of personal information (we do not sell data)
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

12. International Users

ShedSync is operated in the United States. If you are located outside the U.S., your information will be transferred to and processed in the United States.
