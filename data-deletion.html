How to Request Deletion of Your ShedSync Data

Your Privacy Matters: You have the right to request deletion of your personal data at any time. This page explains how to do that.

Overview

If you want to delete your ShedSync account and all associated data, you have several options. All deletion requests are processed within 30 days.

What Gets Deleted

When you request account deletion, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Account Information: Email, password, business name, phone number, address
  • Inventory Data: All building listings, photos, pricing information
  • Website Content: Your public dealer website and all customizations
  • Settings: Color schemes, business hours, social media links
  • Connected Services: Facebook page connections, inventory sync credentials
  • Images: All uploaded building photos and carousel images
  • Location Data: Other lot locations and sync configurations

Note: Billing records may be retained for up to 7 years for tax and legal compliance purposes. This includes transaction history and payment receipts, but does not include your inventory or business content.

Deletion Methods

Method 1: Self-Service Deletion (Easiest)

If you have an active ShedSync account, you can delete your own data:

Step 1: Log in to your ShedSync account at https://shed-sync.com/login.html
Step 2: Go to Settings tab in your Admin Panel
Step 3: Scroll to the bottom and click "Delete Account"
Step 4: Confirm deletion by typing your email address
Step 5: Click "Permanently Delete My Account"
‚úÖ Your account and all data will be deleted immediately. You will receive a confirmation email.

Method 2: Email Request

If you cannot access your account or prefer email:

Step 1: Send an email to privacy@shed-sync.com
Step 2: Include in your email:
  • Subject: "Account Deletion Request"
  • Your account email address
  • Your business name (if applicable)
  • Reason for deletion (optional)
Step 3: We will verify your identity and process the request within 30 days
‚úÖ You will receive a confirmation email once your data has been deleted.

Method 3: Facebook App Data Deletion

If you connected ShedSync through Facebook and want to delete your data:

Step 1: Go to your Facebook Settings ‚Üí Apps and Websites
Step 2: Find "ShedSync" and click "Remove"
Step 3: This will disconnect your Facebook page, but to delete your ShedSync account data, also email us at privacy@shed-sync.com
‚úÖ We will delete your Facebook access tokens immediately and your full account within 30 days.

Deletion Timeline

  • Self-Service Deletion: Immediate (within minutes)
  • Email Requests: Within 30 days of verification
  • Facebook Disconnection: Tokens deleted immediately, full account within 30 days

Export Your Data Before Deletion

If you want to keep a copy of your data before deleting your account:

  1. Log in to your ShedSync account
  2. Go to the Manage Buildings tab
  3. Click "Export Inventory" to download a CSV file
  4. Download any photos you want to keep from the image manager

Your Rights

Under various privacy laws (including GDPR and CCPA), you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (Right to be Forgotten)
  • Portability: Export your data in a machine-readable format
  • Restriction: Request limitation on processing of your data

For more information about how we handle your data, please see our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.
