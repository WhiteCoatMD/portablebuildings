⚙️ DNS Configuration Required

Follow these steps to connect your custom domain:

📋 Step-by-Step Guide for GoDaddy (and most other registrars):
  1. Log in to your GoDaddy account and go to "My Products"
  2. Find your domain (e.g., buytheshed.com) and click "DNS" or "Manage DNS"
  3. Add a CNAME record with these exact settings:
Type: CNAME
Name: www
Value/Points to: your-subdomain.shed-sync.com
TTL: 1 Hour (or leave as default)
⚠️ Common Issues:
  • If you see an existing "A" record for "www", delete it first before adding the CNAME
  • Make sure you're adding the record for "www" not "@" (root)
  • Don't include "http://" or "https://" in the Value field
✓ Step 4: Forward Your Root Domain (Recommended)

So visitors can reach your site at both buytheshed.com AND www.buytheshed.com:

• In GoDaddy, go to Domain Settings → Forwarding
• Click "Add Forwarding"
• Forward buytheshed.com → www.buytheshed.com
• Type: Permanent (301)
• Forward only: Yes

This ensures both URLs work perfectly!

⏱️ Timing: DNS changes typically take 5-60 minutes to propagate (sometimes up to 24 hours). We'll automatically verify your domain once the DNS is configured correctly.

💡 Why "www"? Technical limitation: DNS standards don't allow CNAME records for root domains (like buytheshed.com without www). Using www with domain forwarding is the standard industry solution that all major sites use.
